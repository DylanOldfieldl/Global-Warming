Why Climate Change Is An Important Issue

Did you know that 2 in every 3 people worldwide live in regions of severe water scarcity? Even a small increase in global temperatures will destabilise the water cycle and could make water scarcity much worse. climate change can effect rainfall paterns this will disturb the current water cycles around the world increasing the number of droughts and floods in many areas.

The Danger To Rainforests

Rainforests are some of the most precious habitats on the planet. Global warming however places them at risk, over a third of the Amazon rainforest is already threatened by climate change. On top of that, forest destruction, mainly for agriculture is a major cause of climate change, generating an incredible amount of greenhouse gases.
