How Can We Fix The Problem Of Global Warming

It's not impossible for us to make a differance .Our impact on global warming can potentially be greatly reduced if we collectively work together to reduce our carbon footprint.Transport accounts for around a quarter of all greenhouse gas emissions and across the world, many governments are implementing policies to decarbonize travel.A way you can lower your impact on the climate is to lower your dependancy on motor travel, ride a bike or walk. If you need to travel long distances the bus is also an alternative option, lowering the number of vehicles on the road would be a good start to achieving a healthier climate.
